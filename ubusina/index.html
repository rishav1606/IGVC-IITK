About Us

We are a robotics team from IIT kanpur consisting of both undergraduate and graduate students, working in the field of autonomous ground vehicles to participate in the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition (IGVC), held at Oakland university, MI, USA every year in June for 26 years.

P

PAST RECORD

We debuted in IGVC in 2018 and achieved 5th rank in the design competition and 12th rank overall internationally among 36 teams.

L

LEGACY

We were previously the IIT kanpur team for ABU Robocon, where we have achieved 3rd rank nationally among 108 teams.

E

EAGERNESS TO LEARN

We are a group of highly motivated students with a passion for robotics and always try to learn new things through our journey
